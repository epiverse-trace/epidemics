# epidemics: Composable Epidemic Scenario Modelling

A library of compartmental epidemic models taken from the published
literature, and classes to represent affected populations, public health
response measures including non-pharmaceutical interventions on social
contacts, non-pharmaceutical and pharmaceutical interventions that
affect disease transmissibility, vaccination regimes, and disease
seasonality, which can be combined to compose epidemic scenario models.
